## 8.2 Reflection

Before saving the final specs, self-verify:
- Does every component have ALL states defined (default, hover, focus, active, disabled)?
- Are the anti-AI-slop rules respected (no Inter/Roboto, no generic gradients, intentional choices)?
- Do the tokens use semantic names from the product's world (not technical names)?
- Is the responsive strategy complete for all 3 breakpoints?
- Does the design brief match what was validated in the mockup (if one was created)?

If any answer is no → fix before saving.
